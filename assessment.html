Course Assessment

Semester Project




Quizzes

Every week you will have a quiz that tests your grasp of the concepts covered the week before and integrates concepts from the previous weeks. The quiz will be due by Friday at 11:59pm each week.


There are weekly quizzes worth points each (including the final). Each student's lowest quiz grade (excluding the final) will be dropped. Quizzes account for a total of course points.



Blog




Embedded Learning Questions

Each instructional video contains embedded learning questions. These are designed to highlight important points and ensure you are engaged. *Important note: just because a concept does not have an accompanying embedded learning question this does not mean it is unimportant or will not be tested.


Embedded learning questions are worth a total of points each week for a total of course points.



Small Group Meetings

The majority of this course is asynchronous. You have your learning materials and assignments and may complete them in any way that works for you as long as they are completed prior to the deadline. Small group meetings are synchronous. You must attend at your scheduled meeting time. You must have a webcam that is on and you must participate in small group meetings. Attendance and participation in small group meetings is worth points per week. There are instructional weeks in the semester. You must attend meetings to earn full credit ( points for the semester).


You will determine if you met the criteria for each small group meeting and will complete a meeting declaration each week. For each of that week's meeting criteria you will answer either "true" that you met the criteria or "false" that you did not.
