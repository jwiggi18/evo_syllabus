General Genetics - Spring 2021


Every life science discipline and healthcare field depends on thorough familiarity with genetics principles to be intelligently applied and every life on earth is influenced by these disciplines and fields. Students in this course will solidify their understanding of the the central dogma of genetics, broaden their grasp on genomic regulation, and apply basic genetic principles learned in highschool and earlier college courses to developing a more comprehensive understanding of heredity and population genetics.
