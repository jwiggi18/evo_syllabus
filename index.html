Evolution - Fall 2021

Welcome to the exciting discipline of evolutionary biology! As 4000 level, this course will build on your knowledge of genetics, ecology, physiology, and many more. Biological evolution is inherited change in the properties of groups of organisms over the course of generations. Evolutionary biology seeks to understand the diversity arising from these inherited changes and the history that produced such diversity. Thus, this course explores both extant and extinct diversity and processes that produced it. In this course you should be challenged and come away with a clear understanding of what evolution is and what it is not.
